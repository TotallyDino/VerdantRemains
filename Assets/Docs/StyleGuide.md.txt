# Verdant Remains — Style & Mood Guide

## Emotional Tone
- Surreal wonder.
- Uneasy stillness.
- Isolation with subtle company.
- Consequences in quiet.

## Visual Style
- Low-poly, Moebius-inspired.
- URP toon-lit shaders with rim & hatch overlays.
- Inverted hull outlines for clarity.
- Muted natural tones with alien accents.

## World Design
- Suggest, don’t fully reveal.
- Environment acts as character.
- Long shadows, fog, atmospheric haze.

## Narrative Style
- Hybrid presentation drives story.
- Minimal exposition.
- Rewards curiosity & inference.

## Current Status
- Style locked; shaders partially implemented.
- Art assets to follow later phases.
